Once upon a time, in a magical land, also known as Portland, Oregon

There was a house. This house, at some point dubbed "The Treehouse" was in a cool neighborhood, and while it was pretty broken down, it had cheap rent and a great big huge tree, which invited a rotating circus of characters that finally landed on the present-day configuration of Justin Sochan, Leyna Rynearson, Maxwell Hunter, and Mike Misosoup. Justin and Maxwell had been working in a local print shop when we saw the need for custom, quality, cheap stickers with quick turn around. With Maxwell and Justin's combined print and graphic design experience, Mike's head for numbers, and Leyna's almost unhealthy obsession with fantastic customer service, it just seemed right to go into business together. Treehouse Stickers was born! We still proudly print out of the heart of the Alberta Arts district in Portland, Oregon. We love working with small businesses, food production lines, artists, and artisans of all kinds. We've turned stickers into food labels, mother's day art pieces, car decals, instrument decorations, bar codes, packaging, chap book covers, business cards, one-time use stencils, fliers, limited edition art prints, and more. Talk to us - we are always interested in helping you do something cool and unique with your vision. We'll hand deliver to local businesses in the Portland area, and we can ship quickly pretty much anywhere, and we'd be thrilled to add you to our map of places we've shipped to. We also have great recommendations of cool places to get screenprinting, buttons, or an array of other creations.
